Fried Chicken Recipe

Fried Chicken is the GOAT

Fried Chicken for us by us

  • chicken
  • buttermilk
  • spice mix
  • cooking oil
  • pickle juice

Steps

  1. Marinate in buttermilk/pickle juice
  2. heat oil to 375 degrees
  3. fry chicken til internal temp of 150
  4. pat dry with paper towels
